Vocal - Carnatic

What you learn: You learn basics in exercises like Sarali, Jandai, UpperSthaai etc. We train you to improve your voice and sruthi. Upon your excellence in basics, your next level will be Geetham, Varnam and Keerthanam. As confidence of your vocal strength improves, we move upon advanced topics like Pancharathnam, Raagam, Thaanam and Pallavi.

Certifications: Certifications level from Grade 1 to 8.
Chordcraft Institute of Music Certification

Exams: Bridge Academy, Chennai
Government Music Examinations, Tamilnadu

Outcomes: Equivalent to Diploma course.
Participation opportunity in various musical events and TV shows.
Performance Artist

Duration:Based on your performance the duration of course can go upto 6 years

Veena
